About Us!

IUCEE-Student Chapter at Model Institute of Engineering and Technology is a close knit community that aspires to create an environment for students to grow and instil a sense of giving back to the society.

Our Vision

"To create global awareness on Sustainable development and become a hub of world class learning and innovation"

Mission

  • To provide sustainable development solutions for improving the quality of life.
  • To build an ecosystem for materialising ideas & prototypes helpful for our college and society utilizing engineering skills.
  • To create a society where technology helps in preservation of the environment.
